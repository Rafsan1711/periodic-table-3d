Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 3, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Periodic Table 3D. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Key Point: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

1. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent in app, and interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and app performance metrics

2. User-Provided Information

  • Account Information: Username and email (if you create an account)
  • Community Posts: Content you share in forums and discussions
  • Feedback: Ratings, reviews, and support messages

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or files
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect payment information (app is free)

How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide core features and save your preferences
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and enhance user experience
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and fix issues
  • Analytics: To understand which features are most popular
  • Communication: To send important updates about the app (you can opt-out)

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Third-Party Services We Use

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and analytics
    Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Netlify: For web hosting
    Privacy Policy: netlify.com/privacy

We Will Share Your Data Only If:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect rights, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure Firebase Realtime Database with access rules
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to user data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and notifications in app settings
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@periodictable3d.com

Children's Privacy

Periodic Table 3D is designed for educational purposes and may be used by students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information.

Cookies and Tracking

Web Version: We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Mobile App: The app stores preferences locally on your device. No tracking cookies are used.

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 14 months
  • Crash Reports: Deleted after 90 days
  • Community Posts: Retained for historical reference unless deleted by you

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification (for major changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@periodictable3d.com
  • Website: periodic-table-3d.netlify.app
