Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2025

Simple Summary:

Periodic Table 3D is a free educational app. We only collect basic information to improve the app. We never sell your data or show ads.

What Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to make the app work better:

  • Account Information: Your email and username when you sign up (optional for community features)
  • Usage Data: Which features you use to improve the app
  • Device Information: Your device type (Android/iOS) for compatibility

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide access to all features of the app
  • To save your progress and preferences
  • To enable community features (forum posts, comments)
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

What We DON'T Do

  • We DO NOT sell your information to anyone
  • We DO NOT show advertisements
  • We DO NOT track your location
  • We DO NOT share your data with third parties (except Firebase for storage)

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Firebase (Google's secure platform). You can delete your account and all data anytime from the app settings.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for students of all ages. We don't collect any personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of optional features
